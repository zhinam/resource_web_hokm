3 Players

How to Play

At the start of the round, you need to remove one card with a rank of 2 from the deck to ensure the card count remains correct.

The game is then played exactly the same way as in the 4-player version, with one key difference:

  • In 3-player Hokm, there are no teams—each player plays independently for themselves.

The rest of the rules, including choosing the Hakem, selecting the Hokm, and playing each round, remain the same.
